Spreading Positivity in the Workforce

Studies show that employers who strive for a healthy work enviroment often have higher success. And this message has never been so important. People around the U.S report harmful working enviroments, unreasonable deadlines, and harsh management. These behaviors tank productivity in the workforce. As employers and managers, we have a responsibility to our coworkers to cultivate an enviroment they can focus in. An enviroment they enjoy working in. Not only does this boost morale, but our profits and returns have a direct correlation to the happiness of our collegues. This is what "Making Waves" is all about.
